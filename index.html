About

Giovanni Nicolazzo is a researcher at Transcrime, Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, where he completed his PhD in Criminology in 2025. His work focuses on organised and financial crime, criminal infiltration in legitimate markets, corruption, sanctions circumvention, and ownership structures. He develops analytical frameworks that connect criminological interpretation with operational risk detection.

Research focus

Current work investigates how corporate opacity, intermediary ecosystems, and cross-border ownership chains shape illicit opportunity structures. The goal is to identify measurable patterns that support prevention, screening, and evidence-based policy design.
